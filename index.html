What is Bookmark?

A bookmark lets you save your favorite websites in your browser so you can return to them later with a single click. It’s a great way to keep track of important or frequently visited pages. Bookmarks can usually be organized into folders for better management. Most browsers also let you sync your bookmarks across devices. This feature is especially useful for productivity and quick access.
